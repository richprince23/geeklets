WHAT IS STARTUP SCHOOL

Startup School at InnVille plays a pivotal role in nurturing and empowering startups by offering comprehensive training in startup methodologies, digital skills, and vocational/technical expertise. Its primary mission revolves around incubating and supporting these emerging ventures to thrive in today's competitive landscape




OUR ACTIVITIES

Startup School typically engages in various activities aimed at supporting the growth and development of startups, entrepreneurs, and businesses.


Incubation and Acceleration Programs

We offer structured programs designed to support startups at different stages of their growth, providing resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities. We engage our startups in Business Model Development, Prototyping and Product Development, Pitch Preparation, Market Validation and Testing, Financial and Legal Guidance, Mentorship and Guidance and many more.


Community Building

We foster a collaborative and supportive environment that encourages knowledge sharing, networking, and mutual assistance among its members.

Mentorship and Advisory

Access to experienced mentors, advisors, or subject matter experts who provide guidance, advice, and support in various domains such as business development, technology, marketing, and fundraising.

Training and Workshops

Educational programs, skill development workshops, and training sessions aimed at enhancing entrepreneurial skills, business acumen, and technical expertise.

Business Consultancy

Tailored consultancy services support startups, entrepreneurs, and businesses in innovating and achieving lasting growth. We collaborate closely to craft customized strategies aligned with unique goals and market dynamics.
