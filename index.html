Who We Are

InnVille is where innovation meets impact in Ghana. Our hub is a dynamic space nurturing youth talent, supporting startups, and championing innovation. Committed to skills development and economic empowerment, we thrive on inclusiveness and invite partnerships for collective growth. Learn more

OUR SERVICES

InnVille typically offer a range of services to support the growth and development of startups, entrepreneurs, and businesses in its community.



Incubation and Acceleration Programs

We offer structured programs designed to support startups at different stages of their growth, providing resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities. Learn more


Master Class Programs and Bootcamps

We offer targeted and intensive learning experiences tailored to the needs of entrepreneurs, startups, and individuals seeking to learn new skills and innovate. Learn more

Mentorship and Advisory

Access to experienced mentors, advisors, or subject matter experts who provide guidance, advice, and support in various domains such as business development, technology, marketing, and fundraising. Learn more

Community Impact Initiatives

We prioritize our community's welfare, driving various social and environmental projects each year to actively contribute to the enhancement of our local environment and society. Learn more

Empowering Ladies

We recognizes the underrepresentation of ladies in tech and entrepreneurship, so we strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment for female entrepreneurs, innovators, and tech professionals. Learn more

Training and Workshops

Educational programs, skill development workshops, and training sessions aimed at enhancing the soft and hard skills of young people. Learn more

Business Consultancy

Tailored consultancy services support startups, entrepreneurs, and businesses in innovating and achieving lasting growth. We collaborate closely to craft customized strategies aligned with unique goals and market dynamics. Learn more

Smart Schools Project

We collaborate with schools to integrate our digital skills curriculum into their school curriculum, preparing their students for the digital world and ensuring they stay ahead of their colleagues. This program is available at a fee and can be renewed each term. Learn more
