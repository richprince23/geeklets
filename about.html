About Us

InnVille is where innovation meets impact in Ghana. Our hub is a dynamic space nurturing youth talent, supporting startups, and championing innovation. Committed to skills development and economic empowerment, we thrive on inclusiveness and invite partnerships for collective growth. At InnVille, we're passionate about shaping the next wave of African innovators and transformators who will drive impactful change across Africa and beyond. Our dedication to this mission is channeled through four distinct departments; Startup School, Shegeek, Adapter, and Fix - each integral to fulfilling InnVille's overarching vision.


Our Vision

With an unwavering commitment, InnVille envisions fostering a generation of visionary entrepreneurs who will revolutionize industries, empower communities, and spearhead economic growth across Africa, unlocking boundless job opportunities and paving the way for a thriving continent.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide transformative education, resources, and mentorship to young individuals across Africa, empowering them with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to become successful entrepreneurs. Through our comprehensive programs, we aim to foster innovation, promote job creation, and drive sustainable economic development, ultimately building a brighter future for Africa.
