WHAT IS ADAPTER

The Adapter department operates on the principle of 'catching them young.' It is dedicated to equipping young individuals, spanning from senior high school to primary school, with essential soft and hard skills. Our training programs focus on instilling these necessary capabilities, nurturing their growth, and preparing them effectively for future endeavors.




OUR ACTIVITIES

Adapter typically engages in various activities aimed at supporting the growth and development of young people.


Smart Schools Project

We collaborate with schools to integrate our digital skills curriculum into their school curriculum, preparing their students for the digital world and ensuring they stay ahead of their colleagues. This program is available at a fee and can be renewed each term. Register your school


Computer Nti Project

It's a no-cost ICT training program for schools, running for a maximum of 3 days, aimed at introducing students to fundamental ICT concepts. Apply
